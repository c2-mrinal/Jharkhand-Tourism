What is happening

Jharkhand is teeming with events and festivals round the year so be sure to keep track of what happens when
and where so as to be able to enjoy your trip to the fullest. From Cultural fairs like the sharhul to the legendary
carnival and other ritualistic festivals and celebrations and moments , the list is endless.

Momentum Jharkhand

Hon’ble Prime Minister, Narendra Modi has launched the ‘Make in India’ campaign to encourage multi-national, as well as national companies to manufacture their products in India. Inspired from this initiative, Government of Jharkhand has started its own version of Make in Jharkhand campaign and has branded it as “Momentum Jharkhand” .

Sarhul Festival

One of the most popular tribal festivals celebrated across the Jharkhand region, Sarhul festival marks the beginning of New Year. It is celebrated by the Oraon, the Munda and the Ho tribes, of the Jharkhand region. Celebrated every year in the first month of the Hindu calendar, on the third day of the moon or the fortnight of 'Chaitra', .

Carnival

Jharkhand has an annual festival , the best time to travel to jharkhand is during May because this is the time when one witnesses Jharkhand getting decked up for the carnival . Jharkhand carnival is an eagerly awaited occasion where one forgets his worries and tensions and simply plunges into merriment and joy-making for three whole days .

Sports

Jharkhand Olympic Association (JOA) is ready to host the first Sports Meet, which is scheduled to be organized from May 10 to May 12 at Khel Gaon in Ranchi as players from various districts have started to reach the State Capital to participate in the games. The State is going to witness its first State Sports meet with a view to provide better platform to the players .
