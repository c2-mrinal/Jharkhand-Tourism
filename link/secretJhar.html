PERWAGAGH

Perwaghag: One of the most beautiful waterfalls in Jharkhand, hidden in the lush forests near Torpa block. In the rainy season, it is inaccessible. But, in winter around Christmas and New Year, it is one of the most popular picnic spots to enjoy. This is the most beautiful falls in Jharkhand. It is located in Torpa & about 85 km from Ranchi. Torpa is Khunti District and you can reach there in half an hour from Ranchi.
